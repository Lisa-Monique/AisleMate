What is it?

Here are the highlights.

Create

With AisleMate, you can quickly and easily create personalised lists for shopping, packing, recipes and more.

Organize

In a rush? Speed up your shopping trip by organizing your list into the aisle and category order of your favourite supermarkets and stores.

Customize

Choose where you'll be shopping from the included options or create your own! Just input the layout and AisleMate will do the rest.

Sit Back

Last minute family dinner? Forgot to make the cookies for that PTA meeting? Don't even sweat it. Choose your favourite recipe and AisleMate will create the shopping list for you*.

Share

Easily share your lists to allow others to to view and edit them.

Learn More

Sync

Create an account to always have your shoping list with you, manage all your lists on different devices and see your changes on all of them!

Features

AisleMate helps you easily create create, manage and share organized and efficient shopping lists using an easy and intuitive interface. Here's how it works:

  • Quickly select products or choose a recipe and let AisleMate do the work*
  • Enter amount, price and notes and AisleMate will remember for next time
  • Choose or create your personal supermarket layout
  • Share your list to check if you missed anything
  • Calculate the total cost of your shopping list
  • Have a smooth shopping trip!

Our Values

What this brand is really about:

Mission: AisleMate's mission to provide a web application of the highest calibre with an intuitive interface to make shopping as simple as it should be.

Vision: Our vision is a complete replacement of unneccessary, inefficient paper shopping lists with AisleMate as a natural part of everyday life.

About the Creator

My name is Lisa-Monique Edward and I'm a recent community college graduate who hates how long shopping trips take.

Contrary to what you may assume, I have absolutely no background or experience in Computer Science! I created AisleMate as my first full web app for my final project of Harvard University's Introduction to Computer Science Course but more importantly, to relieve the burden of long, inefficient shopping trips involving running back and forth because of forgotten items in certain aisles!
